# Bemy Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2 May 2026

This privacy policy explains how the Bemy mobile application ("Bemy", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use the app. Bemy is a personal pet health tracker for iOS and Android. By creating an account and using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

## 1. Who runs Bemy

Bemy is built and operated by Beebles. You can reach us at **beeble.ptyltd@gmail.com** for any privacy question, request, or concern.

## 2. What we collect

We collect only what is necessary to make the app work, keep your data secure, and diagnose problems.

- **Account information.** Your email address and a password (stored hashed by our authentication provider). We never see your plaintext password.
- **Pet records.** The information you choose to enter about your pets, including names, species, breeds, birthdates, weight, vaccinations, medications, food, vet visits, photos, allergies, and any notes you add. This data belongs to you. We store it so the app can show it to you across sessions and devices.
- **Usage analytics.** Anonymous interaction events such as which screens you visit and which features you use (for example, "logged a vaccination"). These events are tagged with your account's internal user ID but never include the content of your records (no pet names, no vaccine names, no notes, no email).
- **Diagnostics and crash data.** When the app encounters an unexpected error, we record the technical details (stack trace, app version, OS version) so we can fix bugs. Crash reports do not include the content of your records.
- **Device information.** The app version, build number, operating system, and a stable anonymous device identifier used by the analytics tool to group events from the same device.

We do **not** collect: your location, your contacts, your camera roll (we ask for permission only when you tap "add a photo"), your advertising ID, or anything used for ad targeting. Bemy does not show ads.

## 3. Why we collect it

- **Account information** lets you sign in and keeps your data secured to your account.
- **Pet records** are the entire purpose of the app. They exist so you can read them back.
- **Usage analytics** help us understand which features matter and where the app is confusing. We use this to decide what to build next.
- **Diagnostics** help us find and fix bugs. Without crash data, fixing problems would be guesswork.

You consent to the collection above when you tick "I agree to the Privacy Policy" at sign up. You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account. A toggle to disable analytics inside the app is planned for a future release.

## 4. Who else sees your data

Bemy uses a small number of third party processors. We chose each one for security, EU residency where possible, and a clear privacy posture.

- **Supabase (EU region)** stores your account, pet records, and photos. Network traffic uses TLS and Supabase encrypts data at rest.
- **PostHog (EU region)** receives the anonymous analytics events and crash diagnostics described above. PostHog is configured without session recording, without ad network attribution, and without any tracking identifiers.
- **Apple Push Notification Service / Expo Push** delivers the medication and vaccination reminders you have enabled.
- **Expo / EAS** builds and ships the app. They do not see your runtime data.

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with advertisers.

## 5. How long we keep it

- **Pet records and account information** are kept until you delete your account or ask us to delete them.
- **Analytics events** are retained for up to 1 year and then deleted automatically.
- **Crash diagnostics** are retained for up to 30 days and then deleted automatically.
- **Push notification tokens** are kept while your account is active and removed when you delete your account; older tokens become invalid as devices change.

## 6. Your rights

You can:

- **Access and correct** your data. Every record in the app is yours to view and edit.
- **Export** your data. Email us and we will send you a JSON dump within 30 days.
- **Delete** your data. Use Settings → Delete Account in the app, or email us if you can't sign in.
- **Withdraw consent** for analytics at any time by deleting your account.

If you live in the EU or UK, you have additional rights under GDPR: to restrict processing, to object, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. We will respond to any such request within 30 days.

## 7. Children

Bemy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used the app, please email us and we will delete the account.

## 8. Security

Network traffic uses TLS, our hosting provider encrypts data at rest, and row level security policies in the database prevent one user from reading another user's records. No system is perfectly secure; if we become aware of a breach affecting your data we will let you know without undue delay.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. For material changes (anything that adds a new category of data collection or a new third party processor), we will surface a notice in the app the next time you open it.

## 10. Contact

For any privacy question, data export request, deletion request, or concern, email **beeble.ptyltd@gmail.com**. Bemy is a small project and replies are best effort, but we'll get back to you as soon as we reasonably can.
